Principles of Design

Reddit does a great job of utilizing the principles of design. The site does a very good job mapping its controls and not having multiple functions for the same buttons, creating a sort of rythm on the page. On the other hand, there is a button that you can press that shows the post's picture (if the post has a picture) without loading a new web page that also closes the post if you press it again. The way that the content is grouped together makes it easy for the user to read it without having too much information in one place and gives the page a pattern and a sense of unity. It also helps the user understand what content goes with what. The signal-to-noise ratio on Reddit makes it very easy for the user to quickly find what he/she is looking for, as the user can quickly move his or her eyes through the page. The layout is very basic and easy on the eyes which makes the signal-to-noise ratio very high. Reddit is also balanced very well with its colors and the way that it utilizes the rule of thirds. This makes the design of the website appealing to look at.
